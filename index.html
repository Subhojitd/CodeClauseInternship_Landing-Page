You can cancel your reservation by logging into your account and navigating to the "Reservations" section. Select the reservation you want to cancel and follow the cancellation process outlined there.

We accept various payment methods, including credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and online bank transfers. Please check the payment options available during the checkout process.

Yes, Wi-Fi is available in all rooms of our establishment. We provide high-speed internet connectivity to ensure you stay connected during your stay.
